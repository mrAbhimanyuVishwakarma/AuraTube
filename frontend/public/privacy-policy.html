Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

1. Information We Collect

AuraTube only collects information that is absolutely necessary to provide our service. When you log in with Google, we securely receive your email address and profile information. When you choose to download videos to your Google Drive, we request access to your Google Drive.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your Google Drive access is used strictly for creating an "AuraTube Downloads" folder and saving the videos you request. We do not read, modify, or delete any other files in your Google Drive. Your email is used solely to identify your account and track your daily usage limits.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your OAuth tokens are stored securely in our database and are never shared with any third parties. All communication between your browser, our servers, and Google's API is encrypted using industry-standard HTTPS/SSL.

4. Third-Party Services

We use YouTube's API to fetch video metadata, and Google Drive's API to upload files. By using AuraTube, you are also bound by the Google Privacy Policy and YouTube Terms of Service.

5. Your Rights

You can revoke AuraTube's access to your Google Drive at any time by visiting your Google Account's security settings page. Upon revocation, we immediately lose all access to upload files to your drive.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer.
